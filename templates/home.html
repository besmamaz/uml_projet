Our Services

Air Algérie provides you with all the necessary help and assistance whatever your needs.

Seat Service

You can reserve your seat according to your preferences from the cabin map since your reservation and up to 24 hours before the flight, but in order to have a wide choice, it is preferable to make your selection in advance. You can change your choice at any time. This service is offered free of charge on all flights. For reasons and the safety of our passengers on board, vulnerable people such as senior passengers or unaccompanied children cannot be placed in front of the emergency exits.

    Merhaba Service

    Our “Marhaba” service provides any assistance required to simplify and facilitate the journey of our passengers with special needs, whether they are:

    • Unaccompanied minors
    • Pregnant women
    • Passengers traveling with babies
    • Sick passengers
    • Passengers with reduced mobility
    • Passengers who are visually or hearing impaired
    • Passengers requiring additional seats
    • Passengers requiring care during travel
    • Passengers on stretchers
